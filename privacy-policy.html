Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Findmypik. Our platform is designed to provide photographers with a dynamic space to upload and share event-based photos using advanced face recognition technology, allowing users to easily select their pictures from a vast image library. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Notice of Data Collection

We are committed to being transparent about the data we collect and how it is used. When you use Findmypik, we collect information necessary to provide and improve our services. By using our application, you consent to the collection and use of your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

3. Types of Data Collected

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and other identifying information you provide during registration.
  • Photographs: Photos uploaded by photographers for events, including those with identifiable faces.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our application, such as features used, search queries, and preferences.
  • Device Information: Data about the device you use to access our application, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.
